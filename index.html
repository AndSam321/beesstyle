about us

history

Founded by Abbey, also known as "Bee", our journey began with a deep-rooted passion for cooking and a heartfelt desire to serve others. Bee always knew she wanted to cook for others, so in March 2018 when she courageously launched Bee's Style: Egg Rolls & Fried Rice.

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Northwest Arkansas, specifically on the University of Arkansas campus, Bee's Style quickly became a beloved spot in the local community. Bee's Style is dedicated to amazing food made with love, and we are proud to serve our customers with the best food and service possible.

family

From our family to yours, we welcome you with open arms and open hearts! We are eager to be a part of this community and we hope that our food leaves you with a lasting impression. We are proud to serve you and we hope that you enjoy our food as much as we enjoy serving it to you.
